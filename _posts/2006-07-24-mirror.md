Sublime ripples shadow purgatory wanting.<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
This time mirrors my silence.

Wanting and consumed,<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Sanguine waters rushing,<br />
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
My spirit wades to be freed.

A frozen end.
